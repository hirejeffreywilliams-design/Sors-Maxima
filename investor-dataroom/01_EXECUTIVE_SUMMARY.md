## The Opportunity in One Paragraph

SORS MAXIMA is a members-only sports betting intelligence platform that uses a proprietary 51-factor Monte Carlo simulation engine to generate AI-powered pick analysis, parlay construction tools, and real-time odds intelligence for serious sports bettors. The US legal sports betting market crossed $11.3B in gross gaming revenue in 2023 and is projected to reach $40B by 2030. Within that market, a fast-growing segment of bettors (estimated 8-12M Americans) actively seeks analytical tools over tips, presenting a defensible SaaS opportunity in a category with no dominant brand. SORS MAXIMA is fully built, live-ready, and positioned to capture that audience at scale.

## Product Snapshot

| Feature | What It Does |
|---------|-------------|
| **51-Factor Monte Carlo Engine** | Runs up to 1,000,000+ simulations per game across referee bias, travel fatigue, weather, player micro-matchups, coach tactics, and 46 other quantified factors |
| **Quantum Fusion Engine** | Fuses simulation output, CLV tracking, sharp-money signals, and situational analysis into a single pick grade (S / A+ / A / B+ / B / C) |
| **Pre-Game Overdrive** | 3-wave simulation cascade at 24h, 12h, and 2h pre-game; picks deepen in confidence as game approaches |
| **Life Changer Ticket™** | AI-assembled high-leg parlays optimized for underdog exposure with EV and Kelly sizing |
| **Real-Time Odds Hub** | Multi-bookmaker line comparison (10+ books), CLV tracking, sharp signal detection |
| **AI Analyst Companion** | GPT-4o-powered chat grounded in live platform data — answers bankroll, pick logic, and game questions |
| **Bankroll Management** | Kelly criterion, daily caps, and personalized stake recommendations |
| **Admin Intelligence Suite** | Revenue dashboard, fraud detection, retention engine, A/B testing, feature flags, model performance monitoring |
