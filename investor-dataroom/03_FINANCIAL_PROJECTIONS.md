## PART D: ADDITIONAL REVENUE STREAMS (UNLOCKABLE)

### Sportsbook Affiliate Revenue
- **Mechanics:** SORS MAXIMA refers users to partner sportsbooks (DraftKings, FanDuel, BetMGM). Earns $150–$500 CPA per depositing player.
- **Potential at 1,000 subscribers:** If 20% engage with affiliate link = 200 referrals/mo → $30,000–$100,000/mo incremental
- **Impact on valuation:** Affiliate revenue is typically valued at 4–6x EBITDA — highly complementary to the SaaS multiple

### API Licensing / Data Licensing
- **Mechanics:** License simulation output (probability distributions, EV scores, grade signals) to smaller sportsbooks or analytics companies
- **Price:** $2,000–$10,000/month per licensee, depending on scope
- **Realism:** Achievable at Year 2–3 once track record is established

### White-Label Operator Product
- **Mechanics:** License the entire SORS MAXIMA stack (with branding swap) to sportsbook operators, casino groups, or media companies
- **Price:** $10,000–$50,000/month SaaS license + implementation fee
- **Admin suite readiness:** The operator dashboard is already built and functional

## PART E: VALUATION METHODOLOGY

### Method 1 — ARR Multiple (Primary)

| Scenario | Year 3 ARR | Multiple | Value |

| Bear | $324K | 5x | $1.6M |
| Base | $1.5M | 8x | $12.1M |

*Multiple rationale: 5x for early-stage single-digit ARR; 8x for $1M+ ARR with demonstrated retention; 12x+ for hyper-growth with multiple revenue streams. Comparable market: Action Network at ~8–10x ARR at acquisition.*

| Asset | Replacement Cost |

*This establishes a hard floor on valuation — even with zero users, the IP represents $1.5M in equivalent engineering work.*

### Method 3 — Strategic Premium Scenario

A sportsbook paying $500 CAC to acquire bettors, if SORS MAXIMA delivers 5,000 qualified subscribers pre-loaded with betting intelligence: **5,000 × $500 = $25M** in strategic user acquisition value alone.

This explains why sportsbooks (DraftKings, BetMGM, Caesars) pay large premiums for platforms with engaged betting audiences — the customers are pre-qualified and high-LTV.
