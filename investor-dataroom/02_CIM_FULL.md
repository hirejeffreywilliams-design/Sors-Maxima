## SECTION 2: PRODUCT DEEP DIVE

### 2.1 The Core Engine Stack

SORS MAXIMA is built on a layered intelligence architecture. Each layer adds data, signal, and confidence before producing a user-facing output.

**Layer 1 — Data Ingestion**
- Live odds from 10+ sportsbooks via The Odds API
- Game data: ESPN (all sports), BallDontLie (NBA), API-Football (soccer), NHL Stats API, MLB Stats API
- Injury reports, lineup data, weather data, travel schedules
- Sharp-money signals and CLV (Closing Line Value) tracking

**Layer 2 — The 51-Factor Monte Carlo Engine**
The simulation engine processes 51 distinct factors across six categories:

| Category | Example Factors |
|----------|----------------|
| Team Performance | Home/away splits, rest advantage, streak momentum |
| Player Situational | Starter availability, injury impact, hot-hand signals |
| Market Intelligence | Reverse line movement, sharp-money ratios, steam moves |
| Advanced Analytics | Pythagorean win expectation, DVOA, RAPM (sport-specific) |
| Environmental | Travel fatigue scoring, altitude, weather impact coefficients |
| AI-Discovered (NEW) | Referee crew bias, player micro-matchups, coach tactical tendencies, sentiment insider signal, travel quality scoring |

The engine runs Monte Carlo simulations using all 51 factors to generate probability distributions rather than point estimates — providing confidence intervals alongside every prediction.

**Pre-Game Overdrive System:**
- Wave 1: 250,000 simulations at 24 hours before game time
- Wave 2: 500,000 simulations at 12 hours before game time
- Wave 3: 1,000,000+ simulations at 2–3 hours before game time
- Output: Progressive confidence deepening as game approaches, displayed via SimulationDepthIndicator on every pick card

**Layer 3 — Quantum Fusion Engine™**
Fuses simulation output with:
- CLV projections
- Sharp signal indicators
- Situational analysis (trap games, revenge spots, schedule clusters)
- Historical team form engine output
- Precomputed prediction consensus

Output: A single letter grade (S / A+ / A / B+ / B / C) and EV percentage for each pick/leg.

**Layer 4 — AI Reasoning Layer**
OpenAI GPT-4o / GPT-5 is used to:
- Generate natural-language pick explanations grounded in live data
- Power the SORS Intelligence AI Companion (chat interface)
- Write selection reasons for Life Changer Tickets
- Produce the Situational Analysis Engine reports

## SECTION 3: MARKET ANALYSIS

### 3.1 Market Size & Growth

**US Legal Sports Betting Market**
- 2022 GGR: $7.5B
- 2023 GGR: $11.3B (+51% YoY)
- 2024E GGR: $14.8B
- 2030P GGR: $40B+
- Source: American Gaming Association, Morgan Stanley Research

**38 states + DC** have legalized sports betting as of 2026. Additional states (California, Texas) remain active legislative debates — each legalization event creates a step-function increase in addressable users.

**Key Structural Trends:**
1. Mobile-first betting now dominates (~80% of handle is mobile)
2. Parlay wagering growing faster than straight bets (higher margin for books, higher interest from bettors)
3. Player props have become a primary engagement format (NBA, NFL drives heavy volume)
4. "Betting media" (podcasts, newsletters, YouTube) is the fastest-growing sports media category
5. Bettors are actively seeking analytical tools over tips — a shift driven by burned customers from tout services

### 3.2 Target Customer Profile

**Primary (pays for Edge/Max):**
- Age 25–44, male-skewing (80%)
- Bets $200–$2,000/month across multiple sports
- Already uses at least one sportsbook actively
- Has experienced losing money on bad parlays and wants structure
- Consumes betting content (podcasts, Twitter, Reddit r/sportsbook)
- Values data and transparency over vague "locks"

**Secondary (starts at Sharp, upgrades):**
- Casual bettor ($50–$200/month)
- Weekend parlays only, 1–2 sports
- Drawn in by the pick feed and odds comparison tools
- Likely to upgrade after seeing A-grade pick success

### 3.3 Acquisition Channels

1. **Organic / SEO** — Sport-specific content, pick transparency posts, Reddit presence
2. **Referral Program** — Built-in with unique codes, tracked via Stripe webhooks
3. **Sports Twitter/X** — Pick transparency posts, model performance updates
4. **Podcasts / YouTube Integrations** — Sponsorship or ambassador deals
5. **Reddit** — r/sportsbook, r/sportsbetting, sport-specific communities (no spam — genuine value)
6. **Email Re-Engagement** — Smart Retention Sequence Engine handles lifecycle automatically

### 3.4 Regulatory & Legal Positioning

SORS MAXIMA is an **information and entertainment product** — it does not:
- Accept bets or wagers
- Hold player funds
- Operate as a sportsbook
- Provide licensed gambling advice

This classification means **no gambling license is required** in any US jurisdiction. The platform is analogous to Action Network, The Athletic, or FantasyPros — media/analytics products operating inside the broader betting ecosystem without direct regulatory exposure.

**Required disclosures (already present in the platform):**
- "For informational and entertainment purposes only"
- "Must be 21+ in a jurisdiction where sports betting is legal"
- "Not financial or gambling advice"
- Full terms of service, privacy policy, refund policy
