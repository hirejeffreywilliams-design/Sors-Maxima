Goals & constraints

- Primary goal: prevent one person from repeatedly claiming free trials.

- Avoid excessive friction for legitimate users; minimize false positives and business impact.

   - Detect headless browsers, emulators, automation tools, and unusual user-agent patterns.

   - Use behavioral bot detection (mouse/scroll timing, input cadence) and CAPTCHAs as a last resort.



7) Payment & velocity controls

   - Rate-limit trials per payment token and per BIN/country risk tier.

8) Analytics, feedback loop & ML lifecycle
