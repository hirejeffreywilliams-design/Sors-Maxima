Below is an exhaustive checklist of factors that can affect the outcome of a professional sporting event, organized per sport. For each sport I list categories (players/personnel, tactics, physical/environmental, officiating/rules, equipment/technology, preparation/logistics, medical/biological, psychological/social, external/regulatory, randomness/data). Use this as a feature checklist for scouting, modeling, match-prep, or postmatch analysis.



Soccer (association football)

- Players & personnel: individual skill, form, fitness, recent minutes, injuries, suspensions, rotation, bench quality, goalkeeper form, set-piece specialists.  

- Tactics & coaching: formation, pressing intensity, tactical matchups, in-game adaptability, coach decisions, substitutions timing.  

- Team dynamics: chemistry, leadership, captaincy, morale, internal disputes, rotation fatigue.  

- Physical/environmental: pitch surface/quality, dimensions, wet/dry conditions, wind, temperature, altitude, travel fatigue, home crowd, stadium size.  

- Officiating & rules: referee/linesman tendencies, VAR usage/latency, penalty/referee bias history, added-time behavior.  

- Equipment/technology: ball type, boot choice, lighting, communication headsets, GPS tracking accuracy.  

- Preparation/logistics: rest days, training load, recovery protocols, nutrition, accommodation, travel schedule, warmup facilities.  

- Medical/biological: recent knocks, concussion protocols, illness/infection, chronic conditions.  

- Psychological/social: confidence, momentum, crowd hostility/support, media pressure, rivalry intensity.  

- External/regulatory: eligibility, registration/transfer issues, suspension appeals, betting investigations, local laws.  

- Randomness/incidentals: deflections, pitch hazards, bird/interference, freak injuries, red cards.  

- Data/ops: inaccurate live feeds, delayed odds, sensor failures.



Basketball (NBA / FIBA)

- Players & personnel: star availability, minutes load, back-to-back fatigue, bench depth, rookie inexperience, foul trouble.  

- Tactics & coaching: pace, matchups (size/speed), pick-and-roll defense, zone vs man, rotation patterns, late-game playcalling.  

- Team dynamics: chemistry, lineup combinations, hot hand, locker-room issues.  

- Physical/environmental: court surface, arena altitude/temperature, travel schedule, home-court crowd, sightlines for shooters.  

- Officiating & rules: referee whistle tendencies, home bias, replay challenges, foul-calling consistency.  

- Equipment/technology: ball grip, rim stiffness, shot-clock/timing devices, wearable accuracy.  

- Preparation/logistics: practice intensity, recovery (ice/physio), shooting practice, film study.  

- Medical/biological: hamstrings, knee issues, load-management, illness.  

- Psychological/social: momentum swings, trash talk, playoff pressure, clutch experience.  

- External/regulatory: suspensions, eligibility, team fines, trade uncertainty.  

- Randomness: crazy bounces, inadvertent contact, object thrown onto court.  

- Data: scoring/possession miscounts, statkeeper errors.



American Football (NFL / NCAA)

- Players & personnel: QB health/accuracy, OL/DL matchups, RB workload, special teams reliability, backups’ readiness.  

- Tactics & coaching: playcalling balance, 2-minute offense, fourth-down tendencies, play-action use, blitz frequency.  

- Team dynamics: in-game communication, audible usage, coordinator adjustments.  

- Physical/environmental: field turf vs grass, weather (wind/rain/snow), stadium dome vs open, turf hazards, altitude.  

- Officiating & rules: replay overturn rates, pass interference subjectivity, targeting/rule enforcement.  

- Equipment/technology: helmet tech, paging systems, play-calling signals, communication failures.  

- Preparation/logistics: snap counts, practice intensity, pregame warmup, travel.  

- Medical/biological: concussions, ACL/PCL, soft-tissue injuries, cumulative contact effects.  

- Psychological/social: quarterback confidence, home crowd noise, rivalry, penalties from frustration.  

- External/regulatory: eligibility, suspensions, legal issues, roster moves.  

- Randomness: tipped balls, fumble bounce, blocked kicks.  

- Data: playclock errors, stat review inconsistencies.



Baseball (MLB)

- Players & personnel: starting pitcher form/handedness, bullpen depth, hitters’ platoon splits, defensive alignment, catcher pitch-calling.  

- Tactics & coaching: lineup construction, bullpen management, defensive shifts, bunting/sacrifice strategy.  

- Physical/environmental: ballpark dimensions, wind patterns, temperature, humidity, turf vs grass, altitude (Coors Field effect).  

- Officiating & rules: umpire strike-zone tendencies, replay challenges, balk/caught-stealing rules.  

- Equipment/technology: ball composition, bat technology, pitching grips, Statcast accuracy.  

- Preparation/logistics: travel schedule, day/night game effects, bullpen workloads.  

- Medical/biological: arm fatigue/forearm, lat strains, blister issues, recovery between starts.  

- Psychological/social: hitter confidence, slumps, clubhouse dynamics, pressure in pennant races.  

- External/regulatory: roster transactions, eligibility, sign-stealing scandals, disciplinary actions.  

- Randomness: infield bad hops, weather-related delays, throwing errors.  

- Data: scorekeeping subjectivity, pitch-tracking calibration.



Ice Hockey (NHL)

- Players & personnel: goalie form, line chemistry, fatigue from heavy minutes, injuries, penalty-killing/PP units.  

- Tactics & coaching: forecheck strategy, defensive zone coverage, line matchups, physicality.  

- Physical/environmental: ice quality, arena boards/glass condition, travel, altitude.  

- Officiating & rules: obstruction/holding calls, goalie interference interpretations, video review.  

- Equipment/technology: skate sharpening, stick flex, puck behavior, rink lighting.  

- Preparation/logistics: back-to-back games, practice load, travel by bus/plane.  

- Medical/biological: concussions, lacerations, shoulder/knee injuries.  

- Psychological/social: momentum, crowd energy, retaliation dynamics.  

- External/regulatory: suspensions, performance-enhancing drug cases.  

- Randomness: puck bounces, deflections off glass/boards.  

- Data: timing/syncing issues, shot-attempt miscounts.



Tennis

- Players & personnel: player form, fitness, serving/returning form, recent match load, left/right-handed matchups.  

- Tactics & coaching: surface-specific tactics, serve patterns, point-construction, in-match adjustments.  

- Physical/environmental: court surface (grass/clay/hard), ball type, wind, sun glare, temperature, altitude.  

- Officiating & rules: line-calling (Hawk-Eye accuracy), umpire/tiebreak decisions, code violations.  

- Equipment/technology: racket tension, string wear, shoe grip, Hawk-Eye reliability.  

- Preparation/logistics: recovery between rounds, travel across time zones, warmup conditions.  

- Medical/biological: muscle strains, blistering, heat illness, chronic injuries.  

- Psychological/social: mental toughness, crowd support, pressure points (break points).  

- External/regulatory: doping tests, eligibility, withdrawals.  

- Randomness: net-cord luck, sudden weather interruptions.  

- Data: scorekeeping errors, Hawk-Eye calibration.



Cricket (Test/ODI/T20)

- Players & personnel: bowler form/type (pace/spin), batsmen technique, wicket-keeper form, all-rounder impact.  

- Tactics & coaching: field placements, bowling rotations, batting order changes, match tempo.  

- Physical/environmental: pitch condition (turn, bounce), dew, humidity, daylight, ball wear, stadium dimensions.  

- Officiating & rules: umpire LBW standards, DRS/Hawkeye accuracy, over-rate penalties.  

- Equipment/technology: bat composition, ball brand, pitch covers, helmet safety.  

- Preparation/logistics: squad rotation, net practice quality, travel, acclimatization.  

- Medical/biological: fatigue, heat/hydration, stress fractures.  

- Psychological/social: run-chase pressure, sledging effects, captaincy leadership.  

- External/regulatory: pitch reports, crowd interference, eligibility, match-fixing investigations.  

- Randomness: variable bounce, sudden weather.  

- Data: scoring/ball-tracking discrepancies.



Golf (PGA / European Tour)

- Players & personnel: current form, putting stroke, tee accuracy, course fit, caddie influence.  

- Tactics & coaching: shot selection, tee-box strategy, yardage management, course management.  

- Physical/environmental: wind, rain, course condition, greens speed, temperature, altitude.  

- Officiating & rules: rules adjudication (penalty assessments), pace-of-play enforcement.  

- Equipment/technology: club/ball tech, driver adjustability, launch-monitor data.  

- Preparation/logistics: practice rounds, familiarity with course, travel fatigue.  

- Medical/biological: back issues, wrist/elbow injuries, endurance.  

- Psychological/social: pressure on final day, leader-group dynamics, crowd noise.  

- External/regulatory: eligibility, weather suspensions.  

- Randomness: bad bounces, animal/ball interference.  

- Data: shot-tracking errors.



Horse Racing

- Participants: horse fitness/form, breeding, recent races, training regimen, injury history.  

- Jockey & trainer: jockey skill, weight, experience, trainer tactics, stable form.  

- Race conditions: track surface (turf/dirt/synthetic), going (firm/soft), track biases, distance suitability.  

- Starting & pacing: draw/starting gate position, early pace scenario.  

- Physical/environmental: weather, temperature, track maintenance, rain affecting going.  

- Officiating & rules: stewards’ interference calls, whip rules, medication rules.  

- Equipment/technology: horseshoeing, blinkers, gear changes.  

- Preparation/logistics: travel stress, quarantine, stabling.  

- Medical/biological: respiratory issues, tendon injuries, post-race recovery.  

- Psychological/social: horse temperament, crowd effect, jockey–horse rapport.  

- External/regulatory: medication violations, eligibility.  

- Randomness: false starts, interference, bumping.  

- Data: timing errors, photo-finish camera issues.



Motorsport (Formula 1 / NASCAR)

- Drivers & personnel: driver skill, recent performance, experience at circuit, injury/fitness.  

- Vehicle & engineering: setup (suspension, aero), engine performance, tire strategy, reliability, fuel load.  

- Team strategy: pit-stop timing, tire compound choices, safety-car strategy, in-race adjustments.  

- Physical/environmental: track surface, temperature, wind, rain, visibility, altitude.  

- Officiating & rules: penalty system, track limits enforcement, steward decisions.  

- Equipment/technology: tire compounds, electronics, telemetry accuracy.  

- Preparation/logistics: preseason testing, setup time, spare parts availability.  

- Medical/biological: G-force tolerance, dehydration, driver injuries.  

- Psychological/social: teammate dynamics, pressure, qualifying performance.  

- External/regulatory: parc fermé rules, technical directives, penalties for infractions.  

- Randomness: debris on track, collisions, mechanical failures.  

- Data: timing/telemetry glitches.



Boxing & MMA

- Fighters & personnel: weight-cut effects, reach/height, cardio, recent activity, camp quality, corner/team.  

- Tactics & coaching: fight plan, adaptability, clinch strategy, corner advice between rounds.  

- Physical/environmental: ring/cage size, altitude, climate, travel fatigue.  

- Officiating & rules: referee stoppage tendencies, judge scoring patterns, gloves/rules differences.  

- Equipment/technology: glove size, mouthguard, weight-ins, medical checks.  

- Preparation/logistics: sparring intensity, injury management, last-minute changes.  

- Medical/biological: cuts, concussions, weight-cut dehydration, chronic damage.  

- Psychological/social: fighter mindset, intimidation, walkout crowd reaction.  

- External/regulatory: licensing, failed drug tests, commission decisions.  

- Randomness: accidental fouls, slip vs knockdown calls.  

- Data: scoring inconsistencies, replay limitations.



Esports (competitive video games)

- Players & personnel: individual reflexes, ping/latency sensitivity, roster changes, role proficiency.  

- Tactics & coaching: meta adaptation, draft/pick phase, team coordination, communication latency.  

- Physical/environmental: hardware differences, monitor/input latency, venue network stability.  

- Officiating & rules: anti-cheat enforcement, server rules, pause/resume policies.  

- Equipment/technology: PC specs, controller types, network topology, server tick-rate.  

- Preparation/logistics: patch/version differences, practice against similar strategies, travel latency changes.  

- Medical/biological: repetitive-strain injuries, sleep deprivation, vision strain.  

- Psychological/social: tilt, comms breakdown, crowd influence in LAN events.  

- External/regulatory: account bans, match-fixing, patch-induced meta shift.  

- Randomness: server disconnects, packet loss, bug exploits.  

- Data: telemetry/log corruption, inaccurate stat tracking.



Multi-sport / general checklist items (apply to all sports)

- Scheduling: fixture congestion, rest days, short turnaround.  

- Travel & acclimatization: time-zone shifts, jet lag, transport delays.  

- Financial/institutional: ownership instability, bonus incentives, midseason trades/transfers.  

- Legal/regulatory: eligibility rulings, visas, appeal outcomes.  

- Media & information: leaks, opponent scouting published, social-media influence.  

- Integrity & betting: match-fixing, suspicious patterns, investigations.  

- Venue & crowd safety: evacuations, security incidents, crowd interruptions.  

- Technology/data integrity: feed delays, sensor drift, human transcription errors.  

- Health crises: pandemics, quarantines, travel bans.  

- Environmental disasters: wildfires, storms, earthquakes causing postponements or altered conditions.
