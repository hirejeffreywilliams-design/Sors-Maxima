Operational & infrastructure

- Cost monitoring & alerts: set budgets, cloud cost dashboards, and alerts for runaway infra costs.

- Backups & disaster recovery: automated DB & object-store backups, restore drills, RTO/RPO targets.

- Rate limits & quotas: throttle external APIs and user requests; graceful degradation strategies.

- Auto-scaling policies & limits: avoid runaway scale causing cost spikes; set max nodes and scale-down windows.

- Feature flags: rollout, rollback, A/B testing, and emergency kill-switch for risky features.



Data & model ops

- Model versioning & registry: record model versions, training data snapshot IDs, and hyperparameters.

- Data lineage & reproducibility: store raw data snapshots, preprocessing steps, and seed for random ops.

- Drift detection & alerts: monitor model calibration, prediction distribution shifts, and label delays.

- Model rollback plan: quick revert to previous model if performance degrades.

- Training & inference cost tracking: monitor GPU/CPU usage and latency.



Security, privacy & compliance (extra)

- Secure development lifecycle: SAST/DAST in CI, dependency scanning, and secrets scanning.

- Least-privilege IAM: narrow roles for services and humans; rotate keys regularly.

- PII minimization: avoid storing more user data than necessary; anonymize when possible.

- Jurisdictional logging & data residency: ensure legal storage locations per target markets.

- Fraud detection & anti‑abuse: bot detection, multi-account detection, and staking pattern anomalies.



UX & product details often missed

- Empty states & error states: helpful messaging for no-data, slow connections, and failures.

- Rate of information: prevent information overload on a single screen; progressive disclosure.

- Onboarding for probability literacy: quick tutorial/tooltips explaining EV, variance, and bankroll concepts.

- Undo/confirm UX for ticket submission: double-confirm stakes with estimated loss scenarios.

- Internationalization (i18n) & localization (L10n): currency formats, timezones, translations, and local regulations.



Legal & responsible gambling extras

- Prebuilt disclaimers & consent flows: explicit risk acknowledgment before first suggested ticket.

- Audit trail for user decisions: record versioned suggested tickets and user edits (for disputes/regression).

- Affiliate & partner disclosure: show if linking to sportsbooks yields revenue share.

- Age and geolocation spoofing defenses: detect VPN/proxy misuse.



Testing & QA edge cases

- Fake-data sandbox & replay: deterministic test data and ability to replay historical odds for debugging.

- Simulation of extreme events: odds gap spikes, API outages, mass unlocks, and simultaneous high-volume backtests.

- End-to-end tests for third-party failures: simulate odds provider downtime, payment failures, or KYC provider lag.

- Accessibility (a11y) testing: screen reader support, color contrast, and keyboard navigation.



Performance & reliability

- Idempotency keys for mutation endpoints: prevent duplicate orders/tickets on retries.

- Graceful degradation: fallback UI when model service or odds feed is down (show last-known or explain outage).

- Caching strategy: invalidate caches properly for fast-changing odds; TTLs tuned per market.

- Latency SLAs and monitoring: synthetic checks for odds freshness and end-to-end trade flow.



Business & monetization details

- Clear fee & commission model: display any fees before bet placement.

- Partner integrations & contractual limits: rate limits and SLAs with odds providers; fallback providers list.

- Revenue accounting & reconciliation: daily reconciliation for payouts/affiliate revenues.



Support, documentation & team processes

- Runbooks & on-call playbooks: step-by-step remediation for common incidents.

- Customer support flows & templates: dispute handling, refund policy, and escalation paths.

- Internal docs: API contracts, data schema docs, model docs, runbooks in a searchable wiki.

- SLAs for feature delivery and bug fixes: define response/resolve times per severity.



Analytics & product improvement

- Instrumentation plan: events for every user action (generate, edit, accept, stake, cancel).

- Cohort and funnel tracking: measure conversion from generate→stake and retention post-win/loss.

- Experimentation plan: define KPIs for A/B tests before launching.



Ethics & messaging

- No guarantees language everywhere: clear repeated warnings that suggestions are probabilistic, not guarantees.

- Responsible marketing: avoid targeting vulnerable users or promising “get rich” narratives.
