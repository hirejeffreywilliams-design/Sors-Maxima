Here is a ready-to-use, detailed prompt you can paste into an AI, a marketing consultant brief, or a copywriter to get concrete growth and conversion strategies for your betting app.



Main prompt (use as-is)

```

You are a senior growth marketer and product manager with extensive experience scaling mobile betting and iGaming apps. My app is [brief description: name, target market/countries, key product features, platform(s) iOS/Android/web], and my goal is to rapidly increase new paying users and maximize conversion rate (sign-up → deposit → first wager → repeat wager) while staying compliant with region-specific gambling laws and maintaining high user LTV.



Deliver a prioritized 90-day growth plan with specific, actionable tactics across acquisition, onboarding, activation, monetization, retention, and referral. For each tactic include: objective, target audience segment, exact creative/messaging ideas (examples of ad copy, push/email subject lines, landing page headline and CTA), required product changes or experiments, A/B test setup (hypothesis, experiment design, success metrics), estimated impact (uplift % or KPI change), required budget or engineering effort, timeline, and any legal/compliance considerations.



Focus areas to cover:

- High-ROI paid acquisition channels (meta, programmatic sports audiences, TikTok, YouTube, display/native, affiliates/influencers) with audience targets, bid/creative recommendations, conversion funnel tracking, and expected CAC.

- Organic & content strategies (SEO, editorial betting tips, match previews, newsletters) to drive long-term acquisition and reduce CAC.

- Onboarding & first-deposit optimization (signup flow, friction removal, incentive structure, social proof, verification UX).

- Personalization and segmentation (preference capture, dynamic offers, behavioral triggers).

- Retention & reactivation (push/pull notification calendars, lifecycle email flows with templates, win-loss based re-engagement, in-play bet nudges).

- Product features to increase frequency and AOV (cash-out, boosted odds, micro-betting, combos, subscriptions/season passes, in-app gamification/leaderboards).

- Pricing and promotion strategies (welcome bonus structure, wagering requirements, loyalty tiers, time-limited boosts, referral program with viral mechanics).

- Payments & trust (preferred local payment methods, frictionless KYC, transparent withdrawal processes).

- Measurement & analytics (events to instrument, essential dashboards, cohort LTV models, attribution setup, incrementality testing).

- Fraud, responsible gambling, and compliance guardrails.



End deliverable: a prioritized roadmap (with weekly milestones), 6 concrete A/B test plans ready to run, 6 copy/ad creative examples, and a 30/60/90-day KPI forecast (installs, signups, deposit rate, first-week retention, predicted CAC and LTV). If you need more app specifics (existing metrics, budget, markets), request them and adapt the plan.

```

Act as a UA specialist for betting apps. ```

```
Act as a retention/product growth lead. Create a 90-day plan to lift 7-day retention and weekly wagering frequency using lifecycle campaigns, personalization rules, gamification features, and loyalty mechanics. Include example push/email copy and 5 testable hypotheses.

```
