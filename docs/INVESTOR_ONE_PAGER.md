## The Problem

The U.S. sports betting market is growing 20–25% annually ($15B+ GGR in 2025), yet the intelligence tools available to bettors remain shallow. Most betting analysis tools apply 3–10 simple factors. Professional sharp bettors and institutional investors have access to deep statistical modeling — everyday serious bettors do not.

## The Solution

Sors Maxima gives paying members access to AI-graded daily picks generated by a proprietary 46-Factor Model that runs 10,000+ Monte Carlo simulations per matchup, self-calibrates using a 90-day rolling accuracy window, and publishes only picks that exceed minimum Expected Value thresholds. The platform includes a daily AI-curated high-odds parlay (the Life Changer Ticket), a collectible Intelligence Cards™ gamification system, real-time odds from 6+ sportsbooks, and branded cashout advisory tools (Cashout Engineering™).
