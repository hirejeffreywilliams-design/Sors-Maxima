## MARK-BY-MARK ANALYSIS

### Mark 1: SORS MAXIMA™

**Mark:** SORS MAXIMA™ (standard character mark)
**Meaning:** "Sors" = Latin for "fate/fortune"; "Maxima" = Latin for "maximum/greatest" — combined evokes "maximum fortune" or "greatest prediction capability"

**Trademark Assessment:**
- **Distinctiveness:** FANCIFUL/ARBITRARY — Latin compound with no established meaning in the sports technology market. Fanciful marks are the strongest class of trademark.
- **Conflicting Marks Search:** No registered "Sors Maxima" mark found in any class. The Latin compound is sufficiently distinctive that no phonetically similar marks in sports, gaming, or technology classes were identified.
- **Likelihood of Registration:** HIGH
- **Recommended Classes:**
  - Class 9: Computer software for sports prediction and analytics; downloadable mobile software
  - Class 42: Software as a service for sports prediction and intelligence; computer software design and development
  - Class 41: Entertainment services in the nature of providing sports information and analysis
  - Class 43: Online platform services providing sports prediction intelligence (consider if Class 43 applies)

**Filing Priority:** IMMEDIATE (Priority 1)
**Estimated Filing Cost:** $350 × 3 classes = $1,050

**Brand Development Notes:** "Sors Maxima" has a premium, sophisticated feel appropriate for a professional-grade sports intelligence platform. The Latin naming creates a distinctive brand identity that differentiates from the aggressive, colloquial naming common in sports betting media. This premium positioning supports higher subscription pricing.

### Mark 2: QUANTUM FUSION ENGINE™

**Mark:** QUANTUM FUSION ENGINE™ (standard character mark)
**Short Forms:** QFE™, Quantum Fusion™

**Trademark Assessment:**
- **Distinctiveness:** ARBITRARY/FANCIFUL — "Quantum" applied to a sports prediction computation engine, combined with "Fusion" and "Engine," creates a distinctive arbitrary compound mark. "Quantum" in the context of sports analytics is a fanciful application (no quantum computing is involved; "quantum" serves as an arbitrary intensifier indicating simultaneous multi-factor processing).
- **Conflicting Marks Search:** No registered mark found combining "Quantum Fusion Engine" in any sports, gaming, or software class. "Quantum" marks exist in many categories but none combining with "Fusion Engine" in the sports prediction category.
- **Likelihood of Registration:** HIGH (may require disclaimer of "Engine" as descriptive, which is standard practice and does not diminish the compound mark's protection)
- **Recommended Classes:**
  - Class 42: Computer software; AI-powered sports prediction software
  - Class 9: Downloadable software featuring a multi-factor sports prediction engine

**Filing Priority:** IMMEDIATE (Priority 1)
**Estimated Filing Cost:** $350 × 2 classes = $700

**Importance:** The Quantum Fusion Engine™ brand is the core technology differentiator. It should appear in all technical documentation, press materials, investor communications, and platform interface elements. Owning this term as a registered trademark prevents competitors from using "Quantum Fusion" as a copycat feature descriptor.

### Mark 3: SPORTS BUTTON™

**Mark:** SPORTS BUTTON™ (standard character mark)

**Trademark Assessment:**
- **Distinctiveness:** ARBITRARY — A "button" in the software UI context is a generic interface element. However, "Sports Button" as applied to a comprehensive AI sports prediction system represents an arbitrary use of "button" — the button is a metaphor for the simplicity with which users access the complex prediction engine, not a literal UI button. This arbitrary metaphorical use elevates distinctiveness.
- **Conflicting Marks Search:** "Sports Button" search returns no direct conflicts in the AI sports prediction or sports analytics categories. Several "Button" marks exist in sports merchandise and sports fan experience categories (fan voting buttons, etc.) but in distinct product/service categories.
- **Likelihood of Registration:** HIGH-MEDIUM (some risk from generic "button" element; standard disclaimer of "button" as descriptive may be required)
- **Recommended Classes:**
  - Class 9: Downloadable software featuring AI sports prediction
  - Class 41: Sports information and analytics services

**Filing Priority:** IMMEDIATE (Priority 1)
**Estimated Filing Cost:** $350 × 2 classes = $700

**Brand Strategy:** "Sports Button" is the user-facing consumer brand name for the Quantum Fusion Engine. It communicates simplicity (one button, instant AI intelligence) and power (the button activates a 46-factor AI system). The brand should be prominent in App Store listings, consumer marketing, and social media.

### Mark 4: SHARP MONEY FLOW™

**Mark:** SHARP MONEY FLOW™ (standard character mark)

**Trademark Assessment:**
- **Distinctiveness:** SUGGESTIVE-TO-ARBITRARY — "Sharp money" is an established term in sports betting culture referring to professional bettor action. "Flow" in the financial sense (capital flow direction) is descriptive. However, the compound "Sharp Money Flow" as a branded feature name for an AI detection system within a sports prediction platform is suggestive-to-arbitrary in the platform context.
- **Conflicting Marks Search:** "Sharp Money" is used descriptively in sports betting media but does not appear as a registered trademark for a digital platform feature. No registration found for "Sharp Money Flow" as a compound in any class.
- **Likelihood of Registration:** MEDIUM-HIGH (descriptiveness argument may be raised against "Sharp Money" as established betting terminology; counter with evidence of distinctive use as a product feature name)
- **Recommended Classes:**
  - Class 42: Sports prediction software featuring sharp bettor capital flow detection
  - Class 41: Sports information services featuring professional bettor activity analysis

**Filing Priority:** HIGH (Priority 2)
**Estimated Filing Cost:** $350 × 2 classes = $700

**Importance:** "Sharp Money Flow" detection is one of the most valuable and most distinctive features of the Sors Maxima Sports Button. Registering this mark protects the naming of this feature and prevents competitors from using "Sharp Money Flow" as a copycat feature name.

### Mark 5: 46-FACTOR ANALYSIS™

**Mark:** 46-FACTOR ANALYSIS™ (standard character mark)

**Trademark Assessment:**
- **Distinctiveness:** SUGGESTIVE-TO-DESCRIPTIVE — The mark describes the 46-factor prediction methodology. The specific number "46" combined with "Factor Analysis" creates a suggestive mark with specific numerical distinctiveness. Pure descriptiveness risk is mitigated by the specific number claim — no prior art system uses exactly 46 factors organized in the manner of the Quantum Fusion Engine.
- **Conflicting Marks Search:** No registered mark found combining "46-Factor" with "Analysis" in sports prediction or AI categories.
- **Likelihood of Registration:** MEDIUM (descriptiveness risk; may require showing of acquired distinctiveness; intent-to-use filing establishes priority while use develops)
- **Recommended Classes:**
  - Class 42: Multi-factor AI sports prediction software

**Filing Priority:** HIGH (Priority 2)
**Estimated Filing Cost:** $350 × 1 class = $350

**Importance:** "46-Factor" is a specific, defensible numerical claim that distinguishes the Quantum Fusion Engine from any competitor claiming a different number of factors. Once the 46-factor figure becomes associated with Sors Maxima in the market, the number itself has trademark value.

## ENFORCEMENT AND MONITORING STRATEGY

### Priority Enforcement Scenarios

1. **Copycat Platform Names:** Any sports prediction platform using "Sors Maxima" or similar fanciful Latin compound should be addressed with cease-and-desist.

2. **"Quantum Fusion" Copying:** Any sports prediction service using "Quantum Fusion" or "QFE" as a feature name for their prediction engine should be addressed.

3. **"Sports Button" Copying:** Any platform using "Sports Button" as a product feature name for AI sports prediction should be addressed.

4. **"Sharp Money Flow" Feature Copying:** If competitors adopt "Sharp Money Flow" as their feature name for sharp bettor detection, that specific branded usage should be addressed.

### Monitoring Program
- Monthly USPTO trademark database monitoring
- Quarterly competitive landscape review for new sports prediction platform naming
- App Store and Google Play monitoring for competing app names
