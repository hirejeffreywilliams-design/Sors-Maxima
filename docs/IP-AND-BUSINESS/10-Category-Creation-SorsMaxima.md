### The Existing Category: Sports Pick Services

The current sports prediction market is dominated by:

**Human Handicappers:** Self-proclaimed experts who sell picks for $20–$100 per selection or monthly subscriptions. The industry has an abysmal track record — academic research consistently shows that commercial handicapping services do not outperform random selection at any sustained rate. The business model rewards marketing over performance.

**Basic Statistical Sites:** Covers.com, OddsShark, Action Network, and similar platforms provide odds information, public betting percentages, and statistical summaries. These are data presentation tools — they display information but do not synthesize it into AI predictions.

**The Result:** The sports prediction market is filled with noise, self-promotion, and unverifiable performance claims. Bettors are paying for picks that don't work, from services that don't prove their results, using methodologies nobody explains.
