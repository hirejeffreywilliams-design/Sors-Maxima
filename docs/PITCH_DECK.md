## SLIDE 3: THE SOLUTION

**A proprietary AI platform that gives serious bettors institutional-grade intelligence.**

- **46-Factor Model Analysis™** — Every matchup scored across 46 independently weighted factors, not 5 or 10
- **Monte Carlo Simulations** — 10,000 standard simulations per matchup; 100,000 deep overnight runs
- **Self-Calibrating AI** — The model corrects its own accuracy drift using a 90-day rolling window. It gets better with every game it analyzes
- **Quality Gate** — Only picks exceeding 50% confidence and 5% Expected Value are published. The model suppresses weak signals rather than publishing noise
- **Everything Is Graded** — Every pick receives an A+ through C grade with exact confidence percentages and Expected Value. Members see the model's conviction on every call

> **Speaker Note:** "This isn't a pick service. It's an intelligence engine. We don't have a guy in a room making picks — we have a 46-factor AI model running thousands of simulations per game, grading its own output, and only publishing what meets a statistical threshold. The model is honest about what it doesn't know."
